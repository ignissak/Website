About me

Hi, my name is Jakub. I am 19 years young student who loves working with technology. I started programming in 2018 when I wanted to learn how to make a custom Discord bot to be cool. This is how I taught my first programming language, JavaScript, which I later switched to TypeScript. I wanted to move forward so I decided to learn Java. Java would become useful in future because I wanted to create custom Minecraft plugins. While creating plugins I learned SQL, Python in school and several other things online. In early 2021 I decided to dump Java and head over to learn Kotlin. In my free time, I play around in Photoshop or other Adobe software. I also won a design competition back in 2018. My hobbies are programming, of course, dancing and singing in the shower while nobody listens.
